Fairy Bread

Discription

Fairy bread is a classic dish in the land down under. It is quick, easy, and cheap to make yet an aussie delicacy that is guaranteed to please crowds both young and old.

ingredients

  • 2 slices of soft white bread (or more if doing a crowd).
  • 1 block softend butter
  • 1 bag or jar of 100s and 1000s

Steps

  1. Apply a generous amount of butter to your white bread making sure to cover to the edges.
  2. Sprinkle your 100s and 1000s untill you can no longer see the butter. alternativly you can tip them onto a plate and place the bread butter side down to coat it in them.
  3. Tilt bread up to unsure lose 100s and 1000s are removed. (this is only nesessary if serving to others.)
  4. Place fairy bread onto plate or platter to serve.
  5. Enjoy!
