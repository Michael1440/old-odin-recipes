Vegemite Sandwhich

discription

The Vegemite sandwhich is a aussie classic, it is cheap yet tasty. And id your an aussie its easy to make, if your not, well then follow this recipe and you should be set.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of soft white bread.
  • Vegemite.
  • butter

Steps

  1. Apply a generous amount of butter to both slices of bread.
  2. Using a butter knife gather a small amount of vegemite and start to spread it on one of your slices of bread.
  3. Continue to spread your vegemite across so that the is a farily even and light brown layer across the whole slice of bread.
  4. It is important that you dont use to much vegemite! It should be applied so that you can still easily see the butter beneath.
